What if I've never been to a hackathon before?
Then we're so glad HACK-O-WASP will be your first ever!
We expect you to have some programming or technical experience, but it’s not a requirement.
We’ll have talks, mentors and workshops to help you with your project.

What   accommodation   facility   is   provided?
we’ll be providing both AC and Non-Ac rooms for 2 days that is for 19th april and 20th april whose prices will be updated soon on the website.

What about the meals? How any meals will be provided?
Five meals will be provided by us , which will be including a lunch , a dinner and a breakfast on the next day with two time snacks.

What about the travel expense? Is it ours or yours?
we will not be covering travel expenses. And you have to come on your own.

Do you offer refunds?
Sorry fees you pay is non refundable , unless medical or any other comparable reason is taken into consideration.

Can I put registration slips on hold?
Yes you can put your ticket on hold prior to 15 days before the event.

How will I get my registration slips if I buy them online?
You’ll be provided a serial number at the time of registration, which you have to show to us on the registration desk on the event day.

Is there ID requirement to enter the event?
Yes an ID proof will be required for the entry in the event. It can be your Aadhar card, College ID, Pan ID etc.

What type of payments do you accept?
We accept cash payment as offline medium, and Google pay or paytm as online methods.
